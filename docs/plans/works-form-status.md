## Program notes (EN, max 1000 char — ~700 char)

> Gamma is the primordial source from which the Delta cycle emerges.
> If Delta is the river's mouth — shaped by tributaries, sediment,
> and accumulation — then Gamma is the crystalline spring.
>
> This work brings into focus musical states in transition. Rhythmic
> structures weave an ever-shifting sonic texture from which timbral
> forms arise. As they thin and disperse, they stretch across time
> and gradually unfold as space.
>
> Here, the algorithmic essence of Delta is distilled to its most
> elemental condition — a cry becoming sound — inhabiting space in
> its purest form.
>
> Per *Intero* and Per *Lontano*

## Work presentation (DRAFT EN, max 2000 char)

Fondata sulla wiki cim2026 (`wiki/concepts/`, vedi "Riferimenti wiki" sotto).
Verificare conteggio caratteri in ODT (target < 2000). Da rivedere nella voce

> Gamma is a stereo fixed-media work (7'44"), the most reduced stage of the Delta
> cycle. It is generated by a custom YAML-to-Csound pipeline in which the score is
> not a list of events but a field of tendency masks: ranges of possibility the
> system samples stochastically, event by event.
>
> One parameter governs everything: the rhythmic value. A single integer subdivides
> time, and the same integer indexes pitch, walking through a dense tuning cloud;
> it fixes the Mid/Side spatial arc of each event as a spatial harmonic, a fraction
> 2π/n of a full stereo rotation; and it sets amplitude downstream of pitch. The
> rhythm is self-generating: from a short seed it unfolds through a non-linear
> feedback map, a logistic map in its chaotic regime, that reads its own output
> back as its next input. I choose the seed and the regime, never the individual
> values.
>
> Pitch lives in a Pythagorean tuning, a chain of fifths folded into the octave,
> which I studied from Walter Branchi and here push to 200 intervals per octave. At
> that density neighbouring pitches almost coincide, opening beating zones that
> arise without my direct control. Loudness is treated as perception, not gain:
> dynamics are levels in Phon, compensated per frequency through the ISO 226:2003
> equal-loudness contours.
>
> Each layer carries a musical state, either held static or driven in transition
> between an initial and a final mask, interpolated across its lifespan. The whole
> unfolds in six sections: the slow birth of clusters, a glissando solo, clouds
> thickening into a swarm, a rupture, a single massive cluster, and a closing
> ultra-glissando.
>
> The working time is deferred: specify, render offline, listen, rewrite. That
> deferral is a choice. Listening becomes a judgement on the orbit of the system,
> rather than on the single gesture.

### Spina dorsale (rationale, aggiornata con fatti verificati dal sorgente)

1. **Inquadramento**: fixed-media stereo 7'44", forma ridotta/cristallina del ciclo Delta.
2. **Sistema compositivo**: pipeline YAML→Csound custom; partitura = maschere di tendenza (spazi di possibilità campionati stocasticamente), non valori fissi. Vedi [[ritmo-generatore]].
3. **Specifiche tecniche** (verificate da `raw/GAMMA/`):
   - **Il ritmo è il parametro generatore unico**: lo stesso valore ritmico pilota tempo (`durata_armonica / ritmo`), altezza (offset di lettura in tabella), spazio (arco Mid/Side `2π/ritmo`) e ampiezza (a valle dell'altezza). → [[ritmo-generatore]]
   - **Ritmo autogenerato** per feedback non lineare (mappa logistica `r=3.99` in regime caotico; lettura lookup input←output). → [[mappa-logistica]]
   - **Accordatura pitagorica a 200 intervalli/ottava** (catena di quinte 3/2 ridotta in ottava; fondamentale 32 Hz, 10 ottave → 2000 frequenze). Eredità Walter Branchi (lui a interi), estesa a 200 gradi → zone di battimento emergenti. → [[accordatura-pitagorica]]
   - **Ampiezza compensata ISO 226:2003** (ppp..fff → Phon → dBFS per frequenza): dinamica percettiva. → [[compensazione-isofonica]]
   - **Spazializzazione Mid/Side orbitale** per evento, arco `2π/ritmo`, inviluppo accoppiato alla stessa fase.
   - Render interno `sr = 96000`, stereo, `0dbfs = 1`; export submission 48 kHz / 24-bit.
4. **Struttura formale** — 6 sezioni, 14 layer (nomi correnti da `Gamma.yaml`, issue GAMMA #2 risolta):
   - I. Nascita Lenta di Cluster (154s) — *salita di gamma, scintillii, scampanello*
   - II. solo ripresaglissante (150s) — *solista, glissando, Nubi sparsissime, Nubi Sparse*
   - III. Dalle nubi allo sciame (60s) — *Nubi Sparse verso sciame, sciame*
   - IV. Rottura (80s) — *impulso, impulso grave*
   - V. Cluster (50s) — *cluster* (singola attivazione massiva)
   - VI. Ultra glissando (110s) — *ultra glissando, sciame*
5. **Postura compositiva**: loop di feedback lungo — specifica → render offline → ascolto → riscrittura. Tempo differito scelto, non subìto. → [[stato-musicale]], [[poetica]]
